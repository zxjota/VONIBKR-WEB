Privacy Policy for Konomikara

Last updated: November 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Konomikara (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the “App”). By downloading, installing, accessing, or using Konomikara, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use Konomikara.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information we automatically collect when you use Konomikara.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: email address, username, password, and profile details.
  • Business information: store name, address, tax ID (optional), and product or pricing data used for inventory and sales management features.
  • Payment information: when you purchase premium features, we collect billing details through secure third-party processors. Konomikara does not store full payment card information.
  • Support and communications: messages, inquiries, and attachments you send when contacting our support team.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: features accessed, screens viewed, session duration, crash logs, and performance metrics.
  • Device and technical data: device model, OS version, unique identifiers, IP address, mobile network info, and app version.
  • Location data: approximate location inferred from your IP address, or precise location when you grant permission for location-based services.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: used to maintain sessions, store preferences, and improve your overall Konomikara experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Konomikara and its core functions such as inventory tracking, sales analytics, and supplier management.
  • To process payments, renew subscriptions, and manage account authentication.
  • To communicate with you about your account, transactions, and system updates.
  • To respond to support inquiries and resolve technical issues.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To perform analytics, understand usage trends, and enhance user experience.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulatory obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: trusted vendors who help operate Konomikara, such as hosting services, analytics tools, and email providers. They may only use your data as directed by us.
  • Business transfers: if Konomikara undergoes a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal reasons: when required to comply with law or protect the rights, property, or safety of Konomikara, users, or the public.
  • With your consent: when you explicitly authorize us to share specific information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services and Links

Konomikara may include integrations or links to external services not operated by us. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review the privacy policies of those third-party services before providing any information.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies and local storage technologies help Konomikara remember preferences, maintain login sessions, and measure app performance. You can control cookie settings through your device or browser, but disabling them may limit some app functionality.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no system is completely secure. If we detect a data breach affecting your information, Konomikara will investigate promptly and notify you in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to operate Konomikara, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized using reasonable security measures.

8. Children’s Privacy

Konomikara is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
